Privacy Policy

Tap and Learn Animal

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tap and Learn Animals. We take the privacy of our young users and their parents very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your privacy while using our mobile application.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is designed specifically for children under the age of 13. We are fully committed to complying with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose any personal information from children.

3. Zero Data Collection

  • Personal Data: We do NOT collect, store, or request any personally identifiable information (such as names, addresses, emails, phone numbers, or precise location).
  • Non-Personal Data & Analytics: We do NOT use any third-party analytics tools or collect any usage data. All interactions within the app happen locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Advertising: Our app is 100% ad-free. We do not include any third-party advertising.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, no information is ever shared with, sold to, or processed by third parties.

5. Data Security

As the app functions entirely offline and does not transmit data, your child’s experience remains private and secure within the local environment of your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
